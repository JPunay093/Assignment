Linear Algebra: Start here as it’s fundamental for understanding data structures and algorithms in machine learning.
Resource: “Linear Algebra and Its Applications” by Gilbert Strang
Online Course: Khan Academy’s Linear Algebra
Calculus: Next, focus on calculus, especially differential and integral calculus, which are crucial for optimization in machine learning.
Resource: “Calculus: Early Transcendentals” by James Stewart
Online Course: Coursera’s Calculus: Single Variable
Probability and Statistics: This is essential for data analysis and making inferences from data.
Resource: “Introduction to Probability” by Dimitri P. Bertsekas and John N. Tsitsiklis
Online Course: Coursera’s Statistics with R
Discrete Mathematics: Important for understanding algorithms and data structures.
Resource: “Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications” by Kenneth H. Rosen
Online Course: Coursera’s Discrete Mathematics
Optimization: Key for machine learning algorithms, especially for solving optimization problems.
Resource: “Convex Optimization” by Stephen Boyd and Lieven Vandenberghe
Online Course: Coursera’s Convex Optimization
Numerical Methods: Finally, learn numerical methods for implementing algorithms that solve mathematical problems.
Resource: “Numerical Methods for Engineers” by Steven C. Chapra and Raymond P. Canale
Online Course: Coursera’s Numerical Methods for Engineers
